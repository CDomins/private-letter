Dear Amelia,

Every morning I wake up thinking of the way you smile — the kind that starts slow and then takes over the whole room.

I've been carrying this letter in my heart for a long time, wondering when the right moment would come. Perhaps there is no perfect moment — only this one, right now, where I tell you simply and truly:

You make ordinary days feel like something worth remembering.

I don't need grand gestures or borrowed words. I only know that when you're near, everything feels a little warmer, a little more possible.

If you'll let me, I'd like to keep making you smile.

With all my heart, Your Secret Admirer ♥
